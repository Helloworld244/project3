Kirkland

My Favorite City - Kirkand, WA

Kirkland is a charming city that captivates visitors with its unique blend of small-town charm and modern amenities. Nestled on the shores of Lake Washington, Kirkland offers stunning waterfront views, a vibrant arts scene, and a plethora of outdoor activities. The city has evolved over the years, attracting a diverse population from across the United States and around the globe. As it grows, so does its culinary landscape, boasting an array of exceptional restaurants that cater to all tastes. Kirkland’s well-maintained trails and walkable streets make it an inviting destination for both residents and tourists, offering a perfect balance of natural beauty and urban convenience. Whether you're exploring its scenic parks, enjoying a meal at a cozy café, or simply taking a leisurely stroll through its picturesque neighborhoods, Kirkland's welcoming atmosphere and abundance of attractions make it a favorite place to visit.

Popular Attractions

Mossbay Hall


by Alexas Fotos pixabay.

Mossbay is a local beer hall that caters to all ages, making it a perfect gathering spot for families and friends. With its welcoming outdoor patio, guests are encouraged to bring their dogs and enjoy the fresh air. The spacious venue is ideal for hosting both large and small parties, ensuring there's plenty of room for everyone. Mossbay offers a diverse menu featuring delicious food that appeals to both kids and adults, along with an impressive selection of drinks that will satisfy any palate. Whether you're looking for a casual meal, a place to celebrate, or a relaxed atmosphere to enjoy quality time with loved ones, Mossbay has it all.

https://mossbayhall.com/

Sirena Gelato

by Dominick Vietor pixabay.

Sirena Gelato is a charming hole-in-the-wall gelato shop that delights the entire family, including furry friends, with its exceptional selection of gelato. This cozy spot offers a unique treat for everyone, featuring a special dog gelato that makes it a favorite among pet owners. Despite its small size, Serena Gelato is known for its speedy service, ensuring that even when the line is long, the wait is well worth it. The shop prides itself on high-quality, flavorful gelato, and for an extra kick, you can add a shot of espresso to your treat. Whether you're craving a classic flavor or something more adventurous, Serena Gelato promises a delightful experience for all.

https://sirenagelato.com/locations/

Cactus Restaurants

by Adam Lot pixabay.

Cactus is a renowned Tex-Mex chain in Kirkland, celebrated for its vibrant and flavorful cuisine. Known for its high-quality drinks and exceptional food, Cactus has become a favorite among locals and visitors alike. The menu features a variety of Tex-Mex classics, all crafted with fresh ingredients and bold flavors. Alongside the delectable food, Cactus offers an impressive selection of drinks, including expertly crafted cocktails and a curated list of tequilas and wines. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty meal or a refreshing beverage, Cactus delivers a memorable dining experience in a lively and welcoming atmosphere.

https://www.cactusrestaurants.com/

ChainLine Brewery
by Vlad Victoria pixabay.

Chainline Brewery, recently opened near Google in Kirkland, boasts a unique and charming storefront crafted from an old train car. This brewery offers a fantastic outdoor setting where visitors can enjoy a variety of activities, including playing or watching pickleball on the onsite courts. Its entirely outdoor location makes it easy to drop by whether you're coming off the nearby trail or driving in. The brewery's prime location, coupled with its engaging atmosphere and excellent selection of craft beers, makes it a must-visit spot for both locals and tourists looking to relax and enjoy the outdoors.

https://chainlinebrewing.com/
